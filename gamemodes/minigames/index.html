Minigames

Minigames is a mix of many different smaller game modes. Every minigame has its own unique gameplay.

BedWars is a competitive PvP minigame where players where you must protect your bed whilst trying to
eliminate your opponents on islands in the sky. You can continue to respawn while your bed is safe. If your bed is destroyed, you’re
on your last life! Use the shop on your island to get better items and win.

SkyWars is a competitive PvP minigame where players start off on a separate island and try to
battle each other. The main goal is to be the last person or team alive to win.

TNTRun is a competitive fast-paced minigame where players start on layer of sand and every block that
they step on dissapears. If a player falls through all of the layers they loose the game.

Duels is a simple competitive PvP minigame where players fight in a 1v1 format.

UHC is a competitive minigame where players fight each other in a randomly generated survival-like world.
The main goal is to be the last person or team alive to win.
